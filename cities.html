Before you travel, keep this in mind:

  • Keep copies of your passport and important documents
  • Share your itinerary and contact details with someone you trust
  • Avoid poorly lit or unsafe areas at night
  • Use official taxis or verified ride apps only
  • Keep valuables close, zipped, and out of sight
  • Know the local emergency number before you arrive
  • Stay aware in crowded places and watch your bag
  • Learn a few basic local phrases for help and directions
